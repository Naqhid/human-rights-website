About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to advocating for human rights, equality, and social justice across India. Our mission is to empower marginalized communities, raise awareness, and take action against injustice.

Our Mission

At All India Human Rights Eagle Head Office, we strive to protect and promote fundamental human rights by:

  • Raising awareness on human rights violations.
  • Providing legal aid to those in need.
  • Empowering vulnerable communities through education.
  • Fostering social justice and equality initiatives.
